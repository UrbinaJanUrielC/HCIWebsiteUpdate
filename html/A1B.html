Released: January 12, 1988


Recorded at: 1987


Songwriter: Stock Aitken Waterman


Producer: Stock Aitken Waterman


Genre: Dance-pop


Length: 3:24


Fun Fact 1: The song uses a similar chord structure and melody to Astley's debut single "Never Gonna Give You Up"


Fun Fact 2: In 2019, Astley recorded and released a 'Reimagined' version of the song for his album The Best of Me, which features a new piano arrangement.
